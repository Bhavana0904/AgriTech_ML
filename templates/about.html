About AgriTech

Empowering Farmers with AI-Driven Crop Recommendations

How It Works

AgriTech leverages advanced Random Forest Machine Learning algorithms to analyze soil and weather data, providing farmers with intelligent crop recommendations. Our system processes seven key parameters to determine the most suitable crops for specific conditions.

Key Parameters Analyzed

  • Nitrogen (N) - Essential for leaf growth
  • Phosphorus (P) - Promotes root development
  • Potassium (K) - Enhances disease resistance
  • Temperature - Optimal growing conditions
  • Humidity - Moisture requirements
  • pH Level - Soil acidity balance
  • Rainfall - Water availability

Benefits for Farmers

Increased Yield

Optimize crop selection for maximum productivity

Sustainable Farming

Make data-driven, environmentally conscious decisions

Risk Reduction

Minimize crop failure through informed choices

Our Mission

At AgriTech, we believe in the power of technology to transform agriculture. Our mission is to democratize access to advanced agricultural intelligence, helping farmers of all sizes make informed decisions that lead to better yields, sustainable practices, and increased profitability. By combining cutting-edge machine learning with agricultural expertise, we're building the future of smart farming.
